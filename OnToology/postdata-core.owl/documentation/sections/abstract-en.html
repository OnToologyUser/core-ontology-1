Abstract

This ontology contains classes and properties that provide information related to poetic works, their manifestations and other characteristics. It also provides useful information from related domains to cover different aspects that complete the information related to the poetic work, through sthe definiton of classes like Person, Organization, Event, Place and Role.

This ontology is the core ontology of the European poetry's network of ontologies define in the POSTDATA project and which description is accesible through this link.
All related documentation to the development of the ontology network is accessible at https://github.com/linhd-postdata/Network-of-ontologies.

The core-ontology documentation is accesible at https://github.com/linhd-postdata/core-ontology.

This development is open to receive found suggestions and issues by the users community and the community research through https://github.com/linhd-postdata/core-ontology/issues.
