Abstract

This ontology contains classes and properties that provide information related to poetic works and their manifestations and other characteristics. It also provides transversal information that is ussefull in different specific domains related and covers different aspects to complete the information related with the poetic work The concepts dealt with are Person, Organization, Event, Place and Role. This ontology is the central ontology of the POSTDATA ontology network, a network of ontologies for European poetry.

The description of the ontology network can be consulted through this link.

All the documentation relating to the development of the ontology network is accessible at https://github.com/linhd-postdata/Network-of-ontologies.

The core-ontology documentation is accesible at https://github.com/linhd-postdata/core-ontology.

This development is open to receive suggestions and problems found by the community of users and the research community through https://github.com/linhd-postdata/core-ontology/issues.
